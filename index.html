About

Finance meets technology.

CA S. Rathinagiri is a Chartered Accountant with over 30 years of professional experience, combining financial expertise with application development, business intelligence, automation, and AI.

His current work focuses on Power BI and MIS reporting, Python-based business tools, RPA workflows, and privacy-enabled local LLM systems. He also trains professionals and students in Excel, Power BI, Python, RPA, CAATs, and applied AI through ICAI programs and workshops.

He is a core developer of Harbour MiniGUI (HMG), founded by Roberto Lopez, and administrator of HMGforum.com. Beyond professional work, he explores 3D stereoscopic photography, fractals, browser-based visual experiments, and Tamil writing.
